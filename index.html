Author: Manasa | Date: August 17, 2024

Declutter regularly by removing items you no longer need and use labeled bins for organized storage. Opt for furniture with built-in storage and create designated zones for different activities. Maintain daily tidying to prevent clutter, and clean thoroughly each week. Utilize vertical space with shelves and hooks, and keep surfaces clear by limiting countertop items to essentials. Rotate seasonal items in closets for easy access.
