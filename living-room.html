LIVING ROOM Organization Tips

Author: Manasa | Date: August 17, 2024

Maximize living room space by using multifunctional furniture like ottomans with storage and tall shelving units. Keep surfaces clear with trays and decorative boxes to organize small items, making the room look tidy and spacious. Regularly declutter to avoid accumulating unnecessary items, and use cable organizers to keep wires hidden and untangled. Designate specific zones for different activities, such as a reading nook or entertainment area, to enhance functionality. Utilize vertical space by hanging art or installing shelves, drawing the eye upwards and creating a sense of height. Consider hidden storage options like under-furniture bins or behind-the-sofa tables for a clutter-free look.
