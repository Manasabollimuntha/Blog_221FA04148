Balcony Organisation Tips

Author: Manasa | Date: August 17, 2024

Utilize vertical space with hanging planters and foldable furniture to save room on your balcony. Create a cozy seating area with rugs, cushions, and ambient string lights. Keep the design simple and organized, grouping plants on tiered stands. Add privacy with outdoor curtains or a trellis. Use outdoor storage for items like cushions and tools, and regularly clean the space to maintain a fresh, inviting atmosphere..
